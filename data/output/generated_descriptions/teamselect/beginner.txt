**Team Selection Problem**

This problem is about selecting three teams of six players each from a pool of 11 players. The teams are named Xavier, Yuri, and Zena.

**Main Variables:**

* Xavier, Yuri, and Zena: These are the three teams, each consisting of six players.
* PLAYER: This is the set of all 11 players.
* GOALIE, DEFENCE, and OFFENCE: These are subsets of players with specific roles (goalie, defense, and offense).
* value: This is a table that shows the value or score of each player.

**Basic Restrictions or Rules:**

* Each team must have exactly six players.
* Each team must have at least one goalie, two defense players, and two offense players.
* No more than two players can be shared between any two teams.

**Goal:**

The goal is to select the teams in a way that maximizes the total value or score of all three teams.
